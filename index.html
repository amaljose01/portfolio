// about me

The engineer behind
the pipeline

I'm a Cloud Operations & Automation Engineer with 11+ years of experience across cloud platforms, large-scale SaaS operations, and technical programme delivery. Currently at SAP Labs India, I work across the full breadth of cloud operations — from infrastructure automation and CI/CD to release governance and stakeholder management.

My background spans multiple dimensions: hands-on engineering (Python, Bash, IaC, GitOps), cloud operations (multi-cloud platform management, tenant lifecycle, capacity planning), and programme management (cross-team delivery, Agile execution, KPI tracking, executive communication).

I believe the best platform engineers can do two things equally well: write the automation that eliminates toil, and communicate its value clearly to the business. I try to do both.

Based in Bangalore, India. Open to conversations about cloud operations, automation strategy, and technical programme delivery at scale.
